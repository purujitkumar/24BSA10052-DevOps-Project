About TechNova

TechNova is the annual technical symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at ABC Institute of Technology. Students, faculty members and industry professionals come together to participate in coding competitions, workshops, keynote sessions and innovation showcases. Since schedules, speaker information and announcements change frequently, this website is designed to demonstrate automated deployment through a DevOps pipeline.
